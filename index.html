Subject Profile

Yuri Grom: Service Record

REF: GROM_3302_DECLASSIFIED
CLEARANCE: LEVEL 5 (OVERSEER)

I. The Record

Born in 3256, Yuri Grom began his career as an officer in the Federal Navy, rising through the ranks to Admiral with exceptional distinction. Federal Command considered him for an instructor role — an honour few attain. Before the offer was made, Grom resigned unexpectedly, citing the pervasive corruption he witnessed at the highest levels of the Federation.

His departure was not solitary. A loyal cohort of Federal pilots followed him, and together they established the EG Union — a faction rooted in the principles of order, anti-corruption, and genuine freedom from superpower politics.

II. Career Path

3256 — BIRTH
Born to a military family. Early education at Federal Naval Academy.
3280-3300 — NAVAL SERVICE
Rapid promotion within Federal Navy. Attained rank of Admiral. Command of the 1st Federal Expeditionary Fleet.
3301 — THE DEFECTION
Resigned commission. Established EG Union in the Clayakarma system (then Euryale).
3302 — ASCENSION
Victor of the "Dangerous Games". Recognized as a Galactic Power by the Pilots Federation.
PRESENT — CONSOLIDATION
Leading the Independent movement. Overseeing 963 systems and protecting 700 billion citizens.
