## What is Powerplay?
Powerplay is Elite Dangerous's ongoing territorial conflict system. Powers are powerful individuals who seek to expand their influence across inhabited space. As a pledged CMDR, you carry out activities that earn Merits — the currency of loyalty — in return for rank rewards and system perks.

## Step 1 — Pledging to Yuri Grom
<div class="img-small img-left">

![Yuri Grom Emblem](images/RedBearLogo.png)

</div>   

1. Open your right-hand cockpit panel in game.
2. Navigate to the **Galactic Powers** tab.
3. Find **Yuri Grom** in the list.
4. Scroll to the bottom and select **Pledge Allegiance**.
5. If you were previously pledged to another power, you may have to wait **30 days** before you can switch. If you are unpledged, the effect is immediate.

<div class="clearfix"></div>

> **Powerplay 2.0 Note:** Merits no longer decay! Once you earn a rank, it is permanent. You can progress at your own pace without losing status.

## Step 3 — Your First Week
In your first cycle, focus on earning Merits through fortification. It's the most accessible activity and builds your rank quickly. See the Fortification guide for details.

## Rank Rewards & Perks
As you accumulate Merits you progress through ranks, unlocking better rewards. Pledged CMDRs enjoy significant benefits:

*   **-100% Rebuy Cost:** When killed in Yuri Grom territory, or by a ship pledged to another Power in territory Yuri Grom does not control.
*   **+60% Bounty Payout:** Earn more from all bounty vouchers redeemed in Yuri Grom territory.
*   **+15% Exploration Data:** Increased sales value for exploration data in our systems.
*   **+15% Trade Bond:** Awarded on commodity sales within Yuri Grom held systems.
*   **-30% Weapon Cost:** Reduced cost for weapon modules in our territory.
*   **Full Module Access:** Access to all Powerplay weapon modules once you reach **Rank 100**.
