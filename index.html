Lore & History

About Yuri Grom

Born in 3256, Yuri Grom began his career as an officer in the Federal Navy, rising through the ranks to Admiral with exceptional distinction. Federal Command considered him for an instructor role — an honour few attain. Before the offer was made, Grom resigned unexpectedly, citing the pervasive corruption he witnessed at the highest levels of the Federation.

His departure was not solitary. A loyal cohort of Federal pilots followed him, and together they established the EG Union — a faction rooted in the principles of order, anti-corruption, and genuine freedom from superpower politics.

"Freedom does not mean Anarchy. True freedom is earned through discipline, and protected through strength."
— Yuri Grom

In 3302, Grom led his fleet to victory in the Dangerous Games — a Frontier Developments-sponsored player event that elevated him to Powerplay status. He became the first power created entirely from player efforts, making the movement uniquely community-driven at its core.

Based out of Clayakarma (formerly Euryale), Grom now commands 963 systems and nearly 700 billion citizens. His ongoing conflict with Federation President Zachary Hudson — who labeled him a traitor — continues to define his political legacy.

The Eyes of Leshak player group, which supports Grom today, operates under the motto "Freedom Among the Stars" and organises weekly Powerplay strategy through their Discord and dedicated operations channels.
