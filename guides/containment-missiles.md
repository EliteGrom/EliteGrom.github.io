## What Are Containment Missiles?
![Containment Missile Demonstration](images/bannerenlist.png)

Containment Missiles are Yuri Grom's exclusive Power Module — available only to pledged CMDRs who have reached sufficient rank. They fire a missile that, on hit, **stops and reboots the target's FSD**, preventing any hyperspace jumps for 15–20 seconds.

## Why They Matter
In PvP, escaping via FSD jump is the most reliable last resort. Containment Missiles hard-counter this. A well-timed shot pins down any target and forces them to fight or die. This is arguably the most impactful power-specific weapon in the game.

## How to Obtain
1. Pledge to Yuri Grom and reach **Rank 100**.
2. Travel to a Yuri Grom controlled system with a **Power Contact** at a station.
3. Purchase Containment Missiles (or any other Powerplay module) from the Power Contact.
4. Fit them to a medium or large missile hardpoint on your ship.

> **Important:** If you defect to another Power, you lose access to these missiles and any equipped ones will stop functioning. Keep your pledge active.

## Engineer Upgrades
Like standard missiles, Containment Missiles can be engineered. Recommended modifications:

*   **Sturdy Mount** — more ammo capacity, essential for extended engagements
*   **High Capacity Magazine** — more shots per reload
*   **Drag Munitions** special effect pairs well for multi-system lockdown

## Effective Use
Fire when your target is below 50% hull and starts manoeuvring for escape. At close range the lock time is minimal. Combined with a fast ship that can close distance quickly (FDL or Mamba), you become an effective bounty hunter that nobody can flee from.
