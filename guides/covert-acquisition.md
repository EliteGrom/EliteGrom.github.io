<h2>What is Covert Acquisition?</h2>
<p>Yuri Grom uses <strong>Covert</strong> methods for both expansion (acquisition) and undermining. This means gathering intelligence data and delivering it — a non-combat activity that anyone can contribute to regardless of their combat skill.</p>

<h2>Preparation Phase</h2>
<p>Before a system can be expanded into, it must be <strong>prepared</strong> during the preparation phase of the cycle. For Grom, preparation involves covert data collection missions from Power Contacts, then delivering them to the target system.</p>
<ol>
  <li>Check Discord for the current preparation targets (posted by coordinators at cycle start).</li>
  <li>Visit a Yuri Grom Power Contact and accept <strong>Covert Intelligence</strong> pickup missions.</li>
  <li>Deliver the data packages to a station in the target preparation system.</li>
  <li>Each delivery contributes to the preparation score.</li>
</ol>

<h2>Expansion Phase</h2>
<p>If a system reaches the preparation threshold, it becomes an expansion candidate. CMDRs then run expansion missions from the Yuri Grom HQ area to the target system to complete the takeover.</p>

<div class="callout"><strong>Coordination is everything.</strong> Covert acquisition only works when many CMDRs target the same system simultaneously. Always follow the coordinator-posted target list — solo preparation rarely reaches the threshold.</div>

<h2>What Ships to Use</h2>
<p>Covert missions carry small cargo payloads, so ship size matters less than jump range. Use a fast, high-jump ship like a <strong>Diamondback Explorer, Asp Explorer</strong>, or engineered <strong>Hauler</strong> to minimise travel time between runs.</p>

<h2>Checking Targets</h2>
<p>Expansion targets are posted weekly on the Yuri Grom Discord. Never prepare an unlisted system — it wastes your effort and may conflict with consolidation plans.</p>
