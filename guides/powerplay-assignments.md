<h2>What are Powerplay Assignments?</h2>
<p>Assignments are your primary directive each week. Every Thursday (Cycle Tick), Yuri Grom issues a set of <strong>five specific missions</strong>. Completing these is the most efficient way to climb the ranks.</p>

<div class="callout"><strong>The Golden Rule:</strong> Completing your weekly Assignment grants a <strong>2.5x Multiplier</strong> to ALL merits earned for the rest of that week. <strong>Always finish your Assignment first!</strong></div>

<h2>Recommended Loadout</h2>
<p>A multi-role ship with <strong>Collector Limpets</strong> is highly recommended for these missions. A ship like the <strong>Federal Challenger</strong> or <strong>Python</strong> works perfectly. You will need a Wake Scanner for specific tasks.</p>

<h2>Assignment Protocols</h2>

<h3>1. Rescue Escape Pods (Reinforcement)</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Go to a system marked as <strong>Reinforcing</strong> (Solid Triangle).</li>
  <li>Scan the <strong>Nav Beacon</strong> upon arrival.</li>
  <li>Return to supercruise and look for <strong>Power Wreckage</strong> signal sources.</li>
  <li>Enter the instance, deploy collector limpets, and retrieve all <strong>Occupied Escape Pods</strong>.</li>
  <li>Fly to a station in the same system and talk to the <strong>Power Contact</strong> to hand them in.</li>
</ol>

<h3>2. Sell Rare Goods (Acquisition)</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Use <a href="https://inara.cz/elite/commodities-rare/" target="_blank">Inara.cz</a> to find the closest rare commodities (e.g., Lavian Brandy, Fujin Tea).</li>
  <li>Fly to the source system, dock at the station, and buy the maximum allowed amount.</li>
  <li>Jump to an <strong>Acquisition</strong> system (Hollow White Circle).</li>
  <li>Sell the commodities at the standard <strong>Commodities Market</strong>. You will receive a completion notification when returning to the main Starport Services screen.</li>
</ol>

<h3>3. Collect Bounties (Reinforcement)</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Find a <strong>Reinforcing</strong> system that contains planetary rings with <strong>Resource Extraction Sites (RES)</strong>.</li>
  <li>Enter the RES and hunt pirates as normal.</li>
  <li>The mission will auto-complete once you hit the required threshold—you do not need to turn them in first.</li>
  <li><strong>Note:</strong> Attacking enemy faction ships (even if not wanted) awards 100 merits and a minor 100 CR bounty.</li>
</ol>

<h3>4. Scan Ships and Wakes (Reinforcing)</h3>
<ul>
  <li>This can be completed passively while bounty hunting in a RES.</li>
  <li>Alternatively, equip a <strong>Wake Scanner</strong> and "camp" outside a busy station in a Reinforcing system to scan high-energy wakes.</li>
</ul>

<h3>5. Transport Power Commodities (Undermining)</h3>
<ol>
  <li>Go to a <strong>Stronghold</strong> system (Star icon) and dock at a station or Power Carrier.</li>
  <li>Talk to the <strong>Power Contact</strong> and collect the specific <strong>Undermining Materials</strong>.</li>
  <li>Ensure you pick the correct material—the description will state which Rival Power system it needs to be delivered to.</li>
  <li>Fly to the target <strong>Rival Power System</strong>, dock at a station, and hand them in at the local <strong>Power Contact</strong>.</li>
</ol>

<div class="callout"><strong>Cycle Reset:</strong> Assignments reset every <strong>Thursday at 07:00 UTC</strong>. Failure to complete 5/5 tasks by this time will result in the loss of your 2.5x multiplier for that week.</div>
