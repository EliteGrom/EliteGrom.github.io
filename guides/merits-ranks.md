<h2>The Merit System (Powerplay 2.0)</h2>
<p>Powerplay 2.0 introduced a significant overhaul to how you earn and keep your rank. The most important change: <strong>Merits no longer decay</strong>. Once you earn a rank with Yuri Grom, it is permanent.</p>

<h2>Earning Merits</h2>
<p>Merits are earned through activities that match our Power's ethos:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Reinforcement (Combat/Logistics):</strong> Defending Strongholds and delivering supplies.</li>
  <li><strong>Acquisition (Covert):</strong> Smuggling and intelligence gathering to expand our borders.</li>
  <li><strong>Undermining (Covert):</strong> Sabotaging rival powers through hacking and data theft.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Rank Tiers & Benefits</h2>
<p>Progressing through ranks unlocks the full suite of Yuri Grom rewards:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>-100% Reduced rebuy</strong> when killed by a ship pledged to another Power in territory your Power does not control.</li>
  <li><strong>-100% Reduced rebuy</strong> when killed in Yuri Grom territory.</li>
  <li><strong>+60% Increased bounty payout</strong> in Yuri Grom territory.</li>
  <li><strong>+15% Increased exploration data sales</strong> in Yuri Grom territory.</li>
  <li><strong>+15% Awarded trade bond</strong> on sales in Yuri Grom territory.</li>
  <li><strong>-30% Reduced weapon module cost</strong> in Yuri Grom territory.</li>
  <li><strong>Access to all Powerplay weapon modules</strong> once you reach <strong>Rank 100</strong>.</li>
</ul>

<div class="callout"><strong>The Ultimate Reward:</strong> Rank 100 is the pinnacle of Powerplay service. Reaching this milestone grants you the right to purchase every unique Powerplay module in the galaxy, not just Yuri Grom's Containment Missiles.</div>

<h2>Efficiency</h2>
<p>Since merits are permanent, you can contribute at your own pace. However, coordinating via Discord ensures your efforts are focused on the systems that need them most for the current cycle's tactical goals.</p>
