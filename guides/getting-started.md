<h2>What is Powerplay?</h2>
<p>Powerplay is Elite Dangerous's ongoing territorial conflict system. Powers are powerful individuals who seek to expand their influence across inhabited space. As a pledged CMDR, you carry out activities that earn Merits — the currency of loyalty — in return for rank rewards and system perks.</p>

<h2>Step 1 — Pledging to Yuri Grom</h2>
<img src="images/RedBearLogo.png" alt="Yuri Grom Emblem" style="max-width: 200px; float: right; margin: 0 0 20px 20px;">
<ol>
  <li>Open your right-hand cockpit panel in game.</li>
  <li>Navigate to the <strong>Galactic Powers</strong> tab.</li>
  <li>Find <strong>Yuri Grom</strong> in the list.</li>
  <li>Scroll to the bottom and select <strong>Pledge Allegiance</strong>.</li>
  <li>If you were previously pledged to another power, you may have to wait <strong>30 days</strong> before you can switch. If you are unpledged, the effect is immediate.</li>
</ol>

<div class="callout"><strong>Powerplay 2.0 Note:</strong> Merits no longer decay! Once you earn a rank, it is permanent. You can progress at your own pace without losing status.</div>

<h2>Step 3 — Your First Week</h2>
<p>In your first cycle, focus on earning Merits through fortification. It's the most accessible activity and builds your rank quickly. See the Fortification guide for details.</p>

<h2>Rank Rewards & Perks</h2>
<p>As you accumulate Merits you progress through ranks, unlocking better rewards. Pledged CMDRs enjoy significant benefits:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>-100% Rebuy Cost:</strong> When killed in Yuri Grom territory, or by a ship pledged to another Power in territory Yuri Grom does not control.</li>
  <li><strong>+60% Bounty Payout:</strong> Earn more from all bounty vouchers redeemed in Yuri Grom territory.</li>
  <li><strong>+15% Exploration Data:</strong> Increased sales value for exploration data in our systems.</li>
  <li><strong>+15% Trade Bond:</strong> Awarded on commodity sales within Yuri Grom held systems.</li>
  <li><strong>-30% Weapon Cost:</strong> Reduced cost for weapon modules in our territory.</li>
  <li><strong>Full Module Access:</strong> Access to all Powerplay weapon modules once you reach <strong>Rank 100</strong>.</li>
</ul>
