<h2>What Are Containment Missiles?</h2>
<img src="images/bannerenlist.png" alt="Containment Missile Demonstration" style="width: 100%; margin: 20px 0; border: 1px solid var(--border-hi);">
<p>Containment Missiles are Yuri Grom's exclusive Power Module — available only to pledged CMDRs who have reached sufficient rank. They fire a missile that, on hit, <strong>stops and reboots the target's FSD</strong>, preventing any hyperspace jumps for 15–20 seconds.</p>

<h2>Why They Matter</h2>
<p>In PvP, escaping via FSD jump is the most reliable last resort. Containment Missiles hard-counter this. A well-timed shot pins down any target and forces them to fight or die. This is arguably the most impactful power-specific weapon in the game.</p>

<h2>How to Obtain</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Pledge to Yuri Grom and reach <strong>Rank 100</strong>.</li>
  <li>Travel to a Yuri Grom controlled system with a <strong>Power Contact</strong> at a station.</li>
  <li>Purchase Containment Missiles (or any other Powerplay module) from the Power Contact.</li>
  <li>Fit them to a medium or large missile hardpoint on your ship.</li>
</ol>
<div class="callout"><strong>Important:</strong> If you defect to another Power, you lose access to these missiles and any equipped ones will stop functioning. Keep your pledge active.</div>

<h2>Engineer Upgrades</h2>
<p>Like standard missiles, Containment Missiles can be engineered. Recommended modifications:</p>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Sturdy Mount</strong> — more ammo capacity, essential for extended engagements</li>
  <li><strong>High Capacity Magazine</strong> — more shots per reload</li>
  <li>Drag munitions special effect pairs well for multi-system lockdown</li>
</ul>

<h2>Effective Use</h2>
<p>Fire when your target is below 50% hull and starts manoeuvring for escape. At close range the lock time is minimal. Combined with a fast ship that can close distance quickly (FDL or Mamba), you become an effective bounty hunter that nobody can flee from.</p>
