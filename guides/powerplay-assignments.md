## What are Powerplay Assignments?
Assignments are your primary directive each week. Every Thursday (Cycle Tick), Yuri Grom issues a set of **five specific missions**. Completing these is the most efficient way to climb the ranks.

> **The Golden Rule:** Completing your weekly Assignment grants a **2.5x Multiplier** to ALL merits earned for the rest of that week. **Always finish your Assignment first!**

## Recommended Loadout
A multi-role ship with **Collector Limpets** is highly recommended for these missions. A ship like the **Federal Challenger** or **Python** works perfectly. You will need a Wake Scanner for specific tasks.

## Assignment Protocols

### Rescue Escape Pods (Reinforcement)
1. Go to a system marked as **Reinforcing** (Solid Triangle).
2. Scan the **Nav Beacon** upon arrival.
3. Return to supercruise and look for **Power Wreckage** signal sources.
4. Enter the instance, deploy collector limpets, and retrieve all **Occupied Escape Pods**.
5. Fly to a station in the same system and talk to the **Power Contact** to hand them in.

### Sell Rare Goods (Acquisition)
1. Use [Inara.cz](https://inara.cz/elite/commodities-rare/) to find the closest rare commodities (e.g., Lavian Brandy, Fujin Tea).
2. Fly to the source system, dock at the station, and buy the maximum allowed amount.
3. Jump to an **Acquisition** system (Hollow White Circle).
4. Sell the commodities at the standard **Commodities Market**. You will receive a completion notification when returning to the main Starport Services screen.

### Collect Bounties (Reinforcement)
1. Find a **Reinforcing** system that contains planetary rings with **Resource Extraction Sites (RES)**.
2. Enter the RES and hunt pirates as normal.
3. The mission will auto-complete once you hit the required threshold—you do not need to turn them in first.
4. **Note:** Attacking enemy faction ships (even if not wanted) awards 100 merits and a minor 100 CR bounty.

### Scan Ships and Wakes (Reinforcing)
*   This can be completed passively while bounty hunting in a RES.
*   Alternatively, equip a **Wake Scanner** and "camp" outside a busy station in a Reinforcing system to scan high-energy wakes.

### Transport Power Commodities (Undermining)
1. Go to a **Stronghold** system (Star icon) and dock at a station or Power Carrier.
2. Talk to the **Power Contact** and collect the specific **Undermining Materials**.
3. Ensure you pick the correct material—the description will state which Rival Power system it needs to be delivered to.
4. Fly to the target **Rival Power System**, dock at a station, and hand them in at the local **Power Contact**.

> **Cycle Reset:** Assignments reset every **Thursday at 07:00 UTC**. Failure to complete 5/5 tasks by this time will result in the loss of your 2.5x multiplier for that week.
